Import your backlist from Amazon

Paste your Amazon author page URL (or a single book URL) and Folio pulls titles, blurbs, covers, and series metadata for you. Manuscript text still needs to come from your own files — we can't extract book text from Kindle. This just saves you from typing every title, blurb, and cover URL by hand.

Heads up: Amazon changes their HTML shape from time to time — if a fetch returns unusable data, tell me and I'll re-tune the parser. Cover images point at Amazon-hosted URLs; consider replacing them in the editor for long-term stability.
