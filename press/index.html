Common questions

Is Folio itself changing? Will Free stay free?

Yes. Free stays free — writing, publishing, sharing, and receiving tips at 0% commission are commitments, not tricks. Press only adds optional marketing tools. If you never subscribe, nothing you use today changes.

Why "Indie" and "Imprint" instead of "Pro"?

Because talent isn't the gate. Some incredibly gifted writers stay on Free forever — that's a legitimate creative choice. Press tiers are about how much of your work you want to spend on being visible, not who you are as a writer.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. Cancel from your account settings; you keep Press features until the end of the paid period. No prorated refunds, no cancellation fees. If you're on the yearly plan, we can look at a refund case-by-case within the first 14 days.

Do boost discounts stack across purchases?

Yes. Indie subscribers get 20% off every Featured Boost as long as their subscription is active. Imprint gets 50% off. Discounts apply at checkout automatically.

What if I already bought Boosts and now subscribe?

Currently active boosts continue at their purchased duration. New boosts you buy after subscribing get the discount immediately.

Payment security

All subscriptions are managed via PayPal — Folio never sees or stores your card details. Manage payment methods and cancellations from your PayPal account.

Folio takes 0% of tips and 0% commission on author sales, always.
