Press subscription comp

Grant or revoke a Folio Press subscription without payment. Use for testing your own account, or comping edge cases (accessibility discounts, beta testers, etc.). Writes to folio_user_settings/<uid>.pressSubscription.

Grant comp subscription

Sets the user's pressSubscription.status to ACTIVE with the tier + period you pick. Client sees it on next auth check or hard refresh.

    


    

      
Revoke subscription

      
Sets pressSubscription.status to CANCELLED. User immediately loses Press features on next check. Use for cleanup or when someone's paid subscription lapses. Does not touch PayPal — for real subscribers, cancel via PayPal instead.
